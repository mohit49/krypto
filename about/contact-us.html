Contact Us

The Unova Foundation welcomes inquiries from individuals and organizations interested in its mission, governance, and long-term stewardship of the Unova ecosystem.

Whether your question relates to grants, career opportunities, or general matters, we aim to provide a clear and appropriate point of contact.

Grants & Ecosystem Support

For inquiries related to grants, funding programs, or ecosystem initiatives supported by the Unova Foundation, please contact us with a brief description of your project or area of interest.

We encourage proposals that align with the Foundation's mandate and contribute to the long-term sustainability, integrity, and responsible development of the ecosystem.

grants@unova.io

Careers at Unova Foundation

If you are interested in working at the Unova Foundation, we welcome expressions of interest from professionals with backgrounds in governance, infrastructure, policy, legal, economics, or related fields.

Please include a short motivation and relevant background information when reaching out.

careers@unova.io
