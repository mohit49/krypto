Support

Unova provides multiple support channels depending on the nature of your inquiry.

This page is intended to help you quickly identify the right type of support and point of contact.

While the Unova Foundation focuses on governance and long-term stewardship, the broader Unova ecosystem includes technical and building support resources for those developing on the infrastructure.

info@unova.io

Foundation &
Governance Support

For matters related to the Unova Foundation, governance, grants, careers, or institutional engagement.

Includes:

  • Foundation mandate and governance
  • Grants and funding programs
  • Working at the Unova Foundation
  • Institutional or academic collaboration

Technical Support

Technical support is available for questions related to the Unova infrastructure, protocol usage, and network participation.

Includes:

  • Infrastructure access and configuration
  • Network participation and roles
  • Protocol-level questions
  • General technical clarification

Please note that the Foundation itself does not operate the infrastructure, but coordinates with relevant technical contributors where appropriate.

Building & Developer
Support

Building support is intended for organizations and teams developing solutions on top of Unova.

Includes:

  • Guidance on building on the Unova infrastructure
  • Architectural considerations and best practices
  • Integration questions
  • References to documentation and tooling

This support is focused on enabling responsible and sustainable development rather than providing managed services.

FAQ

What types of support does Unova provide?

Unova offers support across three main areas:

  • Foundation and governance support
  • Technical support related to the infrastructure
  • Building and developer support for those creating solutions on Unova

Each support type has a different scope and response process.
