1. SITE OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Unova is Web3 infrastructure for the real-world economy: it helps automate
trust and verify business data using smart contracts, with privacy preserved
without relying on central authorities.

Official title / positioning (homepage meta): "Privacy-Enabled Web3 Data
Distribution Blockchain" — multi-layer network for privacy-enabled Web3 data
distribution; builders use smart contracts and the UON token.

Marketing line (hero): Unova is a Web3 infrastructure for the real-world
economy that automates trust and verifies business data via smart contracts,
ensuring privacy without relying on central authorities.

Main navigation buckets: About, Solutions, Community, Build. Mega menu + footer
repeat these routes across pages.
