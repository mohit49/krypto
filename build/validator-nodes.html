Validator
Nodes

Validator nodes play a critical role in maintaining the integrity, reliability, and security of the Unova network.

Validators are responsible for processing transactions, validating execution, and ensuring that network activity is recorded accurately and consistently. By operating independently, validators contribute to the neutrality and resilience of the infrastructure.

Role of Validators

Validators support the network by:

  • Verifying transactions and execution results
  • Maintaining consensus across the network
  • Ensuring data integrity and availability
  • Supporting reliable and deterministic operation

The distributed validator structure ensures that no single entity controls network execution.

Who Can Become a
Validator?

Validator participation is open to organizations and technical operators with the capability to maintain secure and reliable infrastructure.

Typical participants include:

  • Infrastructure providers
  • Technology companies
  • Enterprise IT operators
  • Institutional participants
  • Independent professional node operators

Validators are expected to maintain high operational standards and long-term network participation.

Requirements

Running a validator node requires:

  • Reliable server infrastructure and network connectivity
  • Technical expertise in node deployment and maintenance
  • Continuous monitoring and uptime management
  • Alignment with Unova network policies and operational guidelines

Detailed technical specifications and setup documentation are available upon request.

Incentives and Responsibilities

Validators contribute to network stability and, where applicable, may receive incentives linked to network participation and performance.

In return, validators are expected to:

  • Maintain high availability and performance
  • Operate securely and responsibly
  • Follow network governance and operational rules

The validator role is intended for long-term participants committed to the stability of the ecosystem.

Getting Started

Organizations interested in operating a validator node can contact the Unova team for onboarding information, technical
documentation, and current participation requirements.
