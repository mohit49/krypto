01 Coordination - Sovereignty - Trust
Today's coordination systems were not built for multi-party reality. They force organizations to: hand over control of proprietary data, rely on trusted intermediaries and audits, reconcile fragmented processes across systems, and accept limited automation beyond organizational boundaries. Blockchains remove intermediaries, but most were built for crypto-native use cases and fail to support privacy, scalability, and real-world execution.

Setup a Wallet

To connect to the Unova network, deploy or connect your node and configure a non-custodial wallet to manage credentials, approve transactions, and interact with applications and smart contracts on Unova Mainnet.

The UON Token

UON powers the Unova network and is used to pay transaction fees, execute smart contracts, secure the network, and enable decentralized application operations.

Build on Unova

Unova
Solutions

Unova empowers founders, enterprises, and institutions across industries to build decentralized applications that coordinate real-world data, logic, and execution. Designed for both Web2 and Web3 environments, Unova enables builders to retain full control while leveraging privacy-enabled data transmission, scalable execution, and an open ecosystem for innovation.
